The Best Movies According to Nikky

My top 3 movies of all-time.

A Fall From Grace

This is my favourite movie I could watch it countless times
and still feel what i felt the first time i watched it. The story is so beautiful.

Miracle in Cell Number 7

I felt emotional while watching this particular movie.
I love the suspense and the story line, it felt so real.

Countdown

This is an horror movie but i liked it because it wasn't so scary.
I recommend it for people like me who do not exactly like horrific movies.
